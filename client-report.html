General Experience and Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

The most challenging part was working with a design that was not fully specific. I overcame it by communicating more to clarify expectations.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

I really enjoying how we were able to learn these roles directly through hands on experience. I got to see the dynamics in a web development team from all angles, and I was able to learn a lot from it.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

I would improve communication earlier and make design details clearer from the start.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

I feel way more confident now that I've had experience with it first hand. Now that I have a clear idea of a development pipeline, I'd be way more confident joining a development team.
